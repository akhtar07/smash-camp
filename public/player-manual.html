Getting Started — Creating Your Account

The SMASH CAMP app runs entirely in your phone's browser — no download required. Open any browser (Chrome, Safari) and go to:

App Website
smash-camp.vercel.app

Creating a New Account

  1. Open smash-camp.vercel.app in your browser.
  2. You will see the login screen with two tabs at the top: Login and Sign Up. Tap Sign Up.
  3. Enter your Full Name (e.g. Rahul Sharma). This is the name your teammates will see.
  4. Enter your Email Address. Use an email you check regularly.
  5. Create a Password — at least 6 characters. Remember this password.
  6. Leave the Coach Code field empty — that is only for your coach.
  7. Tap the green Create Account button.
  8. You will be taken directly to your Player Dashboard. You are now registered!

✅ No email confirmation needed. Your account is active immediately after signing up. You do not need to check your email or click any link.

⚠️ Save your password. There is no "forgot password" feature in this version. Write your password down or save it in your phone's notes so you don't forget it.

2

Logging In

  1. Go to smash-camp.vercel.app
  2. The Login tab is selected by default.
  3. Enter the Email and Password you used when signing up.
  4. Tap Login. You will go to your Dashboard.

💡 Tip: Add the app to your Home Screen. In Chrome tap the three dots (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen". In Safari tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". The app will then open like a normal app icon.

Marking Your Attendance

Attendance is worth 50% of your overall camp score, so this is important to do every day you come to training.

  1. Open the app and go to your Dashboard.
  2. Find the attendance card — it shows a map pin icon and the text "Mark Today's Attendance".
  3. Tap the green "I'm Here" button.
  4. The card will turn green and show "You're in! 🎉" — attendance is logged.

⚠️ Important: You can only log attendance once per day. Do this as soon as you arrive at the training venue. Once logged, the button disappears and shows your attendance stats instead.

📅 Attendance Streak: If you attend on consecutive days, your 🔥 streak grows. A streak of 3+ days turns orange, 5+ days turns hot orange. Keep the streak alive!

Training Program — All 45 Days

Tap Program in the bottom navigation bar to see the full 45-day training schedule.

What You See

  1. A hero card at the top showing your current phase, today's day number, sessions logged, and days remaining. It has a "Start Today's Session" button.
  2. The program is split into 4 phase sections — Foundation, Build, Peak, Taper — each with a different color.
  3. Each day card shows the day number, which blocks are in it (Warm-up · Main · Cool-down), and how many exercises.
  4. Days you have completed show a green checkmark and your completion percentage.
  5. Today's day has a glowing "TODAY" badge.
  6. Future days are slightly dim but still tappable — you can preview what's coming.

How to Open Any Day's Session

  1. Scroll to find the day you want.
  2. Tap the day card — it will open the full session page.
  3. You can complete exercises from any day, not just today. But your coach tracks today's completions in real time.

Daily Training Sessions

The Session page is where you track your actual training. Each session is divided into three blocks.

Session Blocks

BlockPurposeTypical Duration
🔥 Warm-upPrepare joints and activate muscles10–15 min
💪 MainCore strength and badminton drills30–45 min
🧘 Cool-downStretching and recovery10 min

How to Use the Session Page

  1. Open a session from the Dashboard or Program page.
  2. At the top you see a progress bar showing how many exercises you have completed vs total.
  3. Each exercise card shows: name, sets, reps, rest time, RPE (effort level), and a coaching cue.
  4. Tap the circle button on the left of an exercise to mark it as done. The card turns dark green with a strikethrough.
  5. Tap the red "Watch" button to open a YouTube search for that exercise — see how to do it correctly.
  6. Your progress is saved automatically to the app — your coach can see your completion % in real time.
  7. When all exercises are done, a "Session Complete! 🏆" celebration screen appears.

💡 RPE Guide: RPE means Rate of Perceived Exertion on a scale of 1–10. RPE 4 = easy warmup. RPE 7 = hard but manageable. RPE 9 = near maximum effort. Train at the number shown for best results.

Watch Button (YouTube)

Every exercise has a red Watch button in the top-right corner of the card. Tapping it opens YouTube search results showing how to perform that exercise correctly. Always watch if you are unsure about form.

The Squad — See Your Teammates

Tap Squad from the Dashboard quick access buttons to see everyone in the camp.

What You See

  1. Three stats at the top: Total Players, Here Today (who marked attendance), and Top Streak in the squad.
  2. A "Training Today" section showing avatar circles for every player who has marked attendance today — green border = present.
  3. The Full Roster list sorted by score, showing each player's: streak 🔥, days attended, and average session completion %.

👥 Social motivation: When you see teammates training, it builds accountability. Use this to push yourself — if someone else is showing up and logging sessions, you should too!

Nutrition Tracker

Tap Nutrition in the bottom navigation bar. The camp has a 6-meal protocol and a daily hydration goal of 3 litres.

Logging Meals

  1. Open the Nutrition page.
  2. You will see 6 meal cards for the day, each with a specific time, food recommendation, and a coaching tip.
  3. After eating a meal, tap the green "Log Meal" button on that card.
  4. The card turns green with a checkmark. The circular progress ring at the top updates.
  5. When all 6 meals are logged the header shows "Perfect day! 🎉"

Hydration Tracking

  1. The hydration card on the right shows your current water intake vs the 3L goal.
  2. Every time you drink a glass of water (approximately 250ml), tap the "+250ml" button.
  3. The blue progress bar fills up as you drink.
  4. When you reach 3L, the button changes to "Goal reached! ✓"

⚠️ Note: Nutrition tracking does NOT affect your leaderboard score. It is for your own health and your coach's visibility. But good nutrition directly improves your training completion, which does affect your score!

Leaderboard & Scoring

Tap Scores in the bottom navigation bar to see the full camp rankings.

How Your Score is Calculated

Attendance % — how many days you showed up 50%
Average Session Completion % — how many exercises you complete each day 50%
Final Score (Attendance % × 0.5) + (Completion % × 0.5)

Example

If you attended 30 out of 35 days (86% attendance) and completed an average of 70% of your exercises, your score is: (86 × 0.5) + (70 × 0.5) = 43 + 35 = 78

What You See on the Leaderboard

  1. The MVP card at the top shows the #1 ranked player with their attendance and completion breakdown.
  2. If you are not #1, a "Your Rank" card appears right below the MVP showing your position and score.
  3. The full rankings list shows all players. Your row is highlighted in green with (You) next to your name.
  4. Each row shows a dual-color bar — green = attendance contribution, blue = completion contribution.
  5. Top 3 players get 🥇🥈🥉 medal badges.

Tips for Getting the Best Score

  1. Show up every day. Attendance is 50% of your score. Even if you feel tired, marking yourself present and doing a partial session is better than not showing up.
  2. Complete all exercises in a session. The completion % is the average of all sessions logged. Even 80% completion each day adds up significantly.
  3. Log attendance first thing. When you arrive at the venue, the first thing you do is open the app and tap "I'm Here". Don't forget — you cannot add attendance after the fact.
  4. Maintain your streak. Consecutive days build a 🔥 streak. Keep it going for motivation and to signal commitment to your coach.
  5. Watch the YouTube tutorials. Use the red Watch button if you are unsure how to perform an exercise. Correct form = better training = better results.
  6. Check the Squad page. When you see teammates showing up and completing sessions, use that as motivation to push harder.
  7. Eat and drink on schedule. Even though nutrition doesn't affect the score directly, proper fuelling means you can complete more exercises at higher intensity.
